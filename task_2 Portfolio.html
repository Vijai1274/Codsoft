My Projects

SMART HOME UI Design
The Smart Home Interface project was born out of my fascination with the intersection of technology and convenience. As smart home devices become increasingly prevalent in our daily lives, I saw an opportunity to design a user-friendly interface that would streamline the control and management of these devices, enhancing the overall user experience.
Using Figma, I embarked on a comprehensive design process that involved research, ideation, prototyping, and refinement. I started by conducting research into the current trends in smart home technology and user interface design, gathering insights into user preferences and expectations.

LPG leakage Intimating & Alert System
The LPG Leakage Intimating & Alert System project was conceptualized with a deep understanding of the potential hazards associated with LPG gas leaks, including fire hazards and health risks. Leveraging IoT technology, I sought to develop a solution that would detect gas leaks in real-time and promptly alert users to mitigate potential dangers.
